Insurance & Coverage

Working With Your Insurance

Great Lakes Medical works with most Medicare, Medicaid, and commercial insurance plans. Our goal is to make the process as simple as possible for both patients and providers, while helping you understand your coverage before any equipment is delivered.

When a provider places an order or sends a referral, we verify that your information is current and correct, review your plan’s benefits, and communicate any requirements or patient responsibility prior to delivery.

What We Verify

  • Eligibility and active coverage with your insurance plan
  • Whether the equipment is a covered benefit under your policy
  • Any prior authorization or documentation requirements
  • Applicable deductibles, copays, and coinsurance amounts

Our staff works directly with your referring provider and insurance company to obtain authorizations when required and to help ensure all criteria are met before billing.

Understanding Coinsurance & Out-of-Pocket Costs

Many insurance plans share the cost of covered services between the plan and the subscriber. After any deductible has been met, your plan may pay a percentage of the allowed amount and you may be responsible for the remaining percentage, known as coinsurance.

For example, if your plan covers 80% of an approved charge, the remaining 20% is your coinsurance. We are required by law to make a good-faith effort to collect all copays and coinsurance amounts as outlined by your plan.

Payment Plans & Financial Assistance

We understand that out-of-pocket expenses can be challenging. Great Lakes offers payment plans to help patients manage coinsurance or other balances that are not covered by insurance.

If you have concerns about cost or a potential financial hardship, please contact our office at 517-536-7350 before delivery so we can review available options and discuss our financial hardship policy with you.

Your Role in the Process

  • Provide accurate insurance, contact, and physician information
  • Notify us if your insurance, address, or physician changes
  • Review any estimated out-of-pocket costs we share with you
  • Contact our office if you receive statements or have questions about a bill

Our team is here to help you understand your benefits and to support you throughout the process—from referral and coverage verification through delivery and ongoing care.
