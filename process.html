Our Proven Process for Home Compression Therapy

We coordinate directly with your provider and insurance to simplify the ordering process, so you can focus on your health while we handle the details.

Our Four-Step Process

A structured approach that guides each patient from referral to in-person delivery, fitting, and education.

Step 1

Referral & Patient Verification

Once a provider places an order or sends a referral, Great Lakes will verify the patient information is current and correct.

Step 2

Coverage & Authorization

We will then verify coverage and benefits, and any prior authorization requirements and communicate that information with the referral source.

Step 3

Clinical Coordination

Great Lakes will work with the referring therapist or provider to ensure that all insurance criteria are met and properly documented and obtain an order.

Step 4

Delivery, Fitting & Education

Once an authorization is obtained, or all necessary paperwork is completed and correct, Great Lakes will contact the patient for in-person delivery including fitting, instruction and education.
