Lipedema

Lipedema is a chronic, sometimes painful condition that involves an abnormal accumulation of fat deposits in the arms, legs and hips. It is typically bilaterally symmetrical. It can be genetic or triggered by hormonal changes, especially in women. Typically the feet are not involved.

Lipedema treatment focuses on managing the fat deposit buildup through dietary and lifestyle changes, use of compression and a form of liposuction performed by practitioners specialized in lipedema treatment.
