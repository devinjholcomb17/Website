Daytime Garments

Ready-to-wear and custom daytime garments to help maintain limb volume during daily activities.

Nighttime Garments

Soft, structured options designed for use at night to support continued edema control.

Compression garments are the maintenance element of compression therapy. Once a limb is reduced, a compression garment is fit to maintain the limb at the reduced state. Great Lakes fits standard and custom compression garments as well as other non-elastic support devices from many manufacturers including Jobst, Juzo, L&R, Medi, Sigvaris and others.
