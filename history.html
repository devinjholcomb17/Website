Great Lakes Medical Equipment, Inc. has specialized since 1992 in supplying high-quality compression therapy products and service to patients in Michigan. Great Lakes utilizes trained compression therapy fitters with many years of experience working with lymphatic and venous disorder patients. We provide education and support on an individual basis to enhance the results of treatment and improve the patient's quality of life. Great Lakes is dedicated to servicing the patient and physician or therapist with delivery, support, education, and insurance reimbursement information. In addition to BioCompression compression devices for lymphedema, chronic venous insufficiency, non-healing wounds, arterial insufficiency and deep vein thrombosis, we supply both daytime compression garments and inelastic wraps and well as a variety of nighttime garments.
