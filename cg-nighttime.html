Nighttime Compression Garments

Nighttime compression garments are designed to provide gentle, sustained support while you rest. They help maintain the gains achieved during daytime wear or therapy sessions and can be an important part of a 24-hour lymphedema or edema management plan.

These garments are typically made from softer, more cushioned materials than many daytime options and are structured to be comfortable when lying or sleeping. They may include adjustable straps, foam inserts, or quilting patterns to help distribute pressure evenly.

Great Lakes works with therapists and providers to match nighttime garments to each patient’s limb shape, skin tolerance, and treatment goals.

How Nighttime Garments Are Used

Nighttime compression is usually worn while resting or sleeping, as directed by your provider or therapist. The goal is to support limb volume when you are no longer upright and moving, helping to prevent fluid from reaccumulating overnight.

During your fitting, Great Lakes staff will demonstrate how to apply and remove the garment, review any straps or closures, and discuss how to position the limb for comfort and safety.

Patients should always follow their provider’s guidance regarding how many hours per night to wear the garment and when to pause use—for example, if there are any changes in skin condition, pain, or new medical issues.

Who May Benefit from Nighttime Garments

Nighttime compression garments may be recommended for patients who:

  • Have moderate to severe lymphedema requiring 24-hour volume management
  • Experience increased swelling overnight or upon waking
  • Have completed intensive therapy and need long-term maintenance strategies
  • Require a softer, more cushioned option than daytime garments while sleeping

The choice between daytime, nighttime, or a combination of both types of garments is individualized. Your therapist or provider will determine what is most appropriate for your condition, and Great Lakes will help ensure your garments fit well and are easy to incorporate into your daily routine.
