Parachute Health allows for medical providers to order DME through a simple and seamless online portal. With this, the orders themselves along with documentation and patient demographics can be securely submitted to us. Parachute Health can integrate into virtually any EHR, including Epic, eClinicalWorks, and athenahealth.
